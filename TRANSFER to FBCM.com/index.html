    A Disciplined Investment Formula

    Fields Brothers Capital Management (“FBCM”) is a Dallas based private equity firm that invests in luxury residential developments in exclusive inner-loop locations. FBCM adheres to a disciplined investment formula. The firm acquires only the best locations, implements high quality architecture and finishes, and is meticulous about all aspects of an investments execution. FBCM has a proven track record of investments that set new standards for design and quality within their market while producing higher than projected returns for our investors. FBCM’s leadership consists of real estate and private equity professionals who have experience deploying capital and managing investments for the premier firms in their respective industries.

    Leadership

    C.W. Fields

    Managing Partner

    C.W., co-founder of FBCM, is actively involved in managing the firm’s portfolio and serves as co-chairman of the investment committee. He also leads Fields Residential, a luxury residential development company, which serves as the development arm of FBCM. Fields Residential currently manages more than $32MM in real estate assets under development including condos, townhomes, and apartments. Since beginning his development career at Trammell Crow Residential, the country’s largest apartment developer, C.W. has been involved in the development of more than 3,000 residential units totaling more than $400MM of capitalization and entitled over 55 acres of land for development. Mr. Fields has a B.B.A. in Finance, cum laude, from Texas State University and is a licensed Texas Real Estate Broker.

    Trevor Fields

    Managing Partner

    Trevor is a co-founder of FBCM and currently serves as co-chairman on FBCM’s investment committee. He also holds the position of Minerals and Royalties Strategy Lead for RPM Energy, a team of oil and gas experts exclusive to KKR’s $2B oil and gas master limited partnership. Trevor is involved in directing capital within the oil and gas space in varying deal structures at both the mineral/royalty and working interest well levels. Prior to joining RPM Energy, he was a Vice President at BBVA Compass in the Oil and Gas Financing division. Earlier in his career, Trevor held various roles of increased responsibility with oil and gas operators EOG Resource and Rosetta Resources. He holds a Bachelor’s of Science in Petroleum Engineering from the University of Texas and an M.B.A. from the University of Houston.
