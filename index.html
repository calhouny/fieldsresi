    Profile

    Fields Residential is an independent real estate development company with more than $21MM in assets under development. The company adheres to a disciplined investment strategy enabling it to quickly capitalize on development opportunities throughout its core markets.

    Fields Residential employs both merchant build and long-term hold platforms depending on limited partner profile, asset location, local real estate market dynamics and macro economic conditions. The company's limited partners include real estate private equity firms, family offices, high net worth individuals and Funds sponsored by Fields Brothers Capital Management, LLC ("FBCM").

    Leadership

    C.W. Fields

    Managing Partner

    Mr. Fields has participated in the development of more than 3,000 luxury residential units and lots totaling $374MM capitalization and entitled over 55 acres of land for development. Prior to founding Fields Residential, Mr. Fields served as the Director of NE Development where he was responsible for the origination and development of more than 800 units throughout the southwest. Prior to NE Development Mr. Fields was with Trammell Crow Residential, the nation's largest multifamily developer, where he played an integral role in the development of a portfolio of 2,100 units. Prior to Trammell Crow Residential, Mr. Fields was an investment sales broker with Magellan Commercial Realty. Mr. Fields received a B.B.A. in Finance, cum laude, from Texas State University and is a licensed Texas Real Estate Broker.

    Fields Brothers Capital Management

    Fields Brothers Capital Management, LLC ("FBCM") is a real estate private equity firm that invests in residential real estate assets throughout Texas' primary markets.

    FBCM targets stable, high yield cash dividends backed by new construction and recently stabilized real estate assets.

    Through its relationship with Fields Residential, FBCM is able to acquire positions in new real estate assets at cost rather than market value, consistently creating an additional +/- 150 bps in yield for the firms limited partners.

    FBCM analyzes local real estate market conditions, short and long-term macroeconomic expectations, interest rate exposure and inflationary impacts on each investment at the asset and portfolio levels.
