Fields Residential

Fields Residential is an independent real estate development company with more than $21MM in assets under development. The company adheres to a disciplined investment strategy enabling it to quickly capitalize on development opportunities throughout its core markets.

Fields Residential employs both merchant build and long-term hold platforms depending on limited partner profile, asset location, local real estate market dynamics and macro economic conditions.

The company's limited partners include real estate private equity firms, family offices, high net worth individuals and Funds sponsored by Fields Brothers Capital Management, LLC ("FBCM").
