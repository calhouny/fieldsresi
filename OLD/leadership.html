Leadership

C.W. Fields

Mr. Fields has participated in the development of more than 3,000 luxury residential units and lots totaling $374MM capitalization and entitled over 55 acres of land for development.

Prior to founding Fields Residential, Mr. Fields served as the Director of NE Development where he was responsible for the origination and development of more than 800 units throughout the southwest.

Prior to NE Development Mr. Fields was with Trammell Crow Residential, the nation's largest multifamily developer, where he played an integral role in the development of a portfolio of 2,100 units.

Prior to Trammell Crow Residential, Mr. Fields was an investment sales broker with Magellan Commercial Realty.

Mr. Fields received a B.B.A. in Finance, cum laude, from Texas State University and is a licensed Texas Real Estate Broker.
